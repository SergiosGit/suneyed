Why SunEyed leads are different

Your prospect already did the homework

A typical web form lead gives you a name and a zip code. A SunEyed lead arrives with:

  • 🔆 System size (kW DC)
  • ⚡ Estimated annual production (kWh)
  • 💰 Estimated annual savings ($)
  • 📅 Calculated payback period (years)
  • 📍 Location and roof configuration
  • 🌳 Obstruction mapping (shading analysis)

When a homeowner contacts you through SunEyed, your first call is about confirming details and closing — not educating from scratch.
