How It Works

Three steps to your solar number

No installer visit needed. No personal information required. Get an honest estimate in under 10 minutes.

⚡

Enter your electricity bill

Add your monthly usage (kWh) and rate (¢/kWh). Use exact numbers from your bill or a quick annual estimate.

🏠

Configure your roof

Set panel count, roof pitch, and orientation. Use the compass sensor for precision. Trace trees and chimneys directly on the sun path chart.

💰

See your payback period

Get annual savings in dollars, monthly production vs. usage, and a break-even year — calculated from real local solar radiation data.

Features

Precision tools for a real decision

📡

Real local solar data

NASA/NOAA radiation data from the nearest of 40,000+ weather stations worldwide — not a generic national average.

🌳

Camera obstruction mapping

Point your phone at trees, chimneys, or neighboring rooftops and trace their silhouette directly onto the sun path chart.

🔀

Multi-array roof support

Model east-west split arrays, dormer sections, and different pitches independently — each with its own obstruction profile.

📅

Monthly usage vs. production

Bar chart overlay showing which months you're over- or under-producing, and how net metering affects your annual credit.

⚖️

Net metering support

Model both monthly and annual net metering settlement — the difference can swing your payback by years.

📴

Fully offline

All calculations run on-device. Your electricity usage, roof config, and results never leave your phone.
